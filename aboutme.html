About Satbir

Hello, I'm Satbir Singh. I am a Security Systems Analyst, Power BI Developer, and Automation Specialist with over 7 years of experience helping organizations turn data into meaningful insights and practical business solutions. My career has been built around solving problems, improving processes, and developing technology solutions that make work easier, more efficient, and more informed. I hold a Master of Science in Information, Network, and Computer Security from the New York Institute of Technology (NYIT), Vancouver, where I developed a strong foundation in cybersecurity, data management, networking, and software development. Since then, I have combined my technical knowledge with real-world business needs to deliver solutions that create measurable value.

Currently, I work with Radius Security as a Product-Focused Security Systems Analyst, where I design and develop Power BI dashboards, Power Apps applications, Python automation tools, and enterprise security system integrations. My work spans data analytics, reporting, automation, cloud technologies, and technical operations, allowing me to bridge the gap between business requirements and technical implementation. What I enjoy most is transforming complex information into simple, user-friendly solutions. Whether I am creating an executive dashboard, developing a mobile application for field technicians, automating a manual process, or integrating new technologies into existing systems, I focus on building solutions that are intuitive, scalable, and impactful. Over the years, I have successfully delivered projects including Power BI reporting platforms, cloud security integrations, ERP migration initiatives, custom Power Apps solutions, workflow automations, and Python-based business applications. These projects have helped teams improve visibility, increase productivity, reduce manual effort, and make better decisions through data.

Beyond technical development, I enjoy working closely with stakeholders, understanding business needs, and translating complex technical concepts into practical solutions. Throughout my career, I have collaborated with cross-functional teams, delivered training sessions, created user guides and documentation, and supported the successful adoption of new systems and technologies. Whether introducing a Power BI dashboard, a custom Power App, or a security platform integration, I focus on ensuring users feel confident, informed, and equipped to get the most value from the solutions I build.

I am passionate about continuous learning, innovation, and creating technology that delivers real business value. This portfolio highlights a selection of projects and solutions that demonstrate my experience in business intelligence, automation, application development, and security technology. When I'm not developing solutions, I enjoy hiking, sketching and exploring emerging technologies, learning new tools, and finding creative ways to solve complex challenges. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to connecting and discussing how technology, data, and automation can help organizations achieve their goals.
