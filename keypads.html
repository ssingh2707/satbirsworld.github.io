Keypad Troubles

Keypad Troubles is a Power Platform-based mobile application developed to assist Monitoring Station Operators and Customer Service Representatives with troubleshooting security systems while speaking with customers. The application provides quick access to panel information, troubleshooting procedures, manuals, and support resources, helping operators resolve issues efficiently during live calls. It also includes an integrated AI assistant designed to answer security panel-related questions and provide technical guidance when needed.

Data Management

The application utilizes a dedicated SharePoint-based database structure. Information is organized across multiple SharePoint lists, each dedicated to a specific manufacturer. These lists store panel information, communicator details, product images, manuals, and other technical documentation. By centralizing this information in a single platform, operators can quickly locate the resources they need without searching through multiple systems.

User Interface

The user interface was designed with simplicity and speed in mind. Users can select manufacturers through recognizable brand logos and are then presented with a list of supported panels and communicators, complete with images for easy identification. The application supports both light and dark modes, providing a comfortable experience for employees working both day and night shifts.

Features

One of the application's primary strengths is its ability to provide instant access to panel-specific information. After selecting a panel, users can view images, access keypad shortcuts, review troubleshooting information, and open PDF manuals directly within the application. This allows operators to guide customers through common procedures and resolve technical issues without leaving the platform. In addition, the application includes the ability to create Zendesk support tickets when escalation is required, ensuring a seamless transition from troubleshooting to formal support.

Radius AI

A key component of the platform is Radius AI, a custom GPT-powered assistant developed specifically for security systems and alarm panels. Radius AI was designed to support operators when handling complex calls, particularly during after-hours periods when technical support may not be readily available. The assistant primarily uses information from manufacturer manuals and documentation integrated into the application. When the required information cannot be found within the available documentation, it can also leverage web-based resources to provide additional guidance. This enables operators to receive accurate and relevant answers without interrupting customer interactions.

Project History

The project was originally created to improve efficiency within the Monitoring Station environment by providing operators with immediate access to technical information and troubleshooting shortcuts. Operators can handle as many as 60 calls per day, many involving critical situations related to fire protection, intrusion alarms, and life-safety systems. In these situations, reducing the time spent searching for information can significantly improve response times and customer service quality. The goal was to allow operators to focus on handling emergency situations rather than spending valuable time locating instructions for routine tasks such as arming, disarming, or troubleshooting security systems. Since its introduction, Keypad Troubles has received overwhelmingly positive feedback from Monitoring Station personnel and has expanded beyond its original purpose. In addition to Monitoring Station Operators, the application is now actively used by Customer Service and Sales teams, demonstrating its effectiveness as a company-wide knowledge and support platform.
