Parts Pro

Part Pro is power platform phone app build to shop parts from Radius Parts department. I build it to make shopping for parts easy and convenient for technicans, customer service and part department.I created it to replace old methods of finding part number from Radius Parts database and ordering parts which involved emailing a word form.

Data

Parts Pro is connected using a SQL gateway to integrate with the Radius Inventory database, allowing users to search via names and purchase parts seamlessly.It tell user what we have in hand and user can track their order and get notification to pickup when it's ready with loaction of the shelf it's on.

User Interface

The user interface was designed with simplicity and usability as key priorities. Users can personalize their experience by selecting from multiple theme options, easily track the status of their orders, and review order details both before and after submission to ensure accuracy and transparency.

Price Calculation

The application retrieves pricing data directly from the database and utilizes DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) queries to perform accurate cost calculations. Pricing is dynamically determined based on factors such as order quantity, ensuring that volume-based pricing rules are applied correctly. Additionally, the system calculates installation costs by considering the resources and expenses associated with each installation, providing an accurate estimate of the total cost to the company. This automated approach improves pricing accuracy, reduces manual calculations, and supports informed decision-making.

Parcel Tracking

In addition, a dedicated Power BI dashboard was developed for the Parts Department. This dashboard enables staff to efficiently monitor, manage, and update order statuses as parts are received, improving operational visibility and streamlining the order fulfillment process.

Project History

This was the first Power Apps project I developed using a SQL database as the primary data source. The project evolved through multiple development and testing phases, with several versions released to incorporate user feedback, improve functionality, and address changing business requirements. The solution ultimately progressed to production and is currently used across four departments. While the technical development presented its own challenges, the most complex aspect of the project was aligning the requirements and obtaining approval from all stakeholders involved. Coordinating feedback, ensuring the solution met the needs of each department, and securing final approval required significant collaboration and project management effort. The successful deployment demonstrated the application's ability to streamline processes and support cross-departmental operations.
